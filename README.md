## About Strategic Flow

Strategic Flow is a technical newsletter copywriting service for SaaS startups. We combine deep technical expertise with a human-in-the-loop AI process to produce newsletters that actually get read.

**The problem we solve:**
- Newsletters that sound robotic and land in spam
- Feature announcements written like internal memos
- CTAs that tell instead of pull

**The Strategic Flow method:**
1. **Data-driven audit** we analyse your current emails and find the gaps
2. **Human-in-the-loop AI writing**  AI drafts, a human expert refines every word
3. **CTR optimisation**  subject lines and CTAs engineered to convert
