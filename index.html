The Strategic Flow Method

How every teardown works

Each teardown follows the same diagnostic framework — applied to the actual email, not a generic template. Every rebuild is deliverable HTML, not a wireframe.

Step 01
Fetch and parse the source URL. Extract subject, hook, body structure, images, and CTA.
Step 02
Score the original on 10 conversion criteria: hook tension, subject specificity, CTA ownership, and cohesion.
Step 03
Identify 3–5 specific flags: exactly what fails and where in the reader's journey it breaks down.
Step 04
Rebuild the full HTML email: same brand, new conversion architecture. Hook, stat cards, body, Before/After, CTA.
Step 05
Generate 3 A/B subject line variants with angle, reasoning, and predicted open rate range.
Step 06
Produce a 4-week content calendar: 8 follow-up email topics built from the same source narrative.
