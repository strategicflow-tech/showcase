// The engine

Three systems.
One assessment.

Strategic Flow Architecture combines structural audit, conversion rebuild, and ROI projection into a single diagnostic engine. Every email your company sends gets scored, diagnosed, and rebuilt.

01 — Structural Audit

The 7-bug diagnostic

Every email is scanned for the 7 structural failures that kill open rates before the reader even decides: filing label subject, caveat opener, feature-first language, flat hierarchy, zero claims, weak CTA, buried contrast. Each bug is named, explained, and scored.

02 — Conversion Rebuild

Consequence-first architecture

The Strategic Flow Method rebuilds every email from the subject line down: consequence-first hook, ownership CTA, before/after contrast in the body, stat cards for major releases. Output is HTML ready to ship — not suggestions to consider.

03 — ROI Projection

Delta in revenue terms

Every rebuild comes with a projected open rate delta based on industry benchmarks and your actual subscriber count. The number makes the conversation with leadership straightforward — it's not "better emails," it's "26,000 additional readers per month."
