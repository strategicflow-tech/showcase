Process

The Strategic
Flow Method

A four-step process that treats every broken email the way a developer treats a broken codebase — find the root cause, fix the architecture, ship something that works.

🔬
Step 01

Audit

Diagnostic scoring across subject, hook, CTA, narrative arc, consequence framing, and cohesion. Root cause identified.

🏗️
Step 02

Rebuild

Complete reconstruction using conversion architecture. Not a patch. A system rebuild from subject line to CTA.

🧪
Step 03

Test

Three A/B variants with predicted performance. Each variant tested against conversion mechanics, not opinion.

🚀
Step 04

Ship

Rebuild-ready HTML, segmented audience outputs, and a 4-week calendar. Everything you need to press send immediately.
